Garage Lab – AI Assistant

Exploring the power of voice and AI in daily workspaces

Context

Garage Lab is a research initiative focused on enhancing productivity through AI-powered assistants in creative studios. This project documents the ideation, design, and prototype of a desktop-based AI assistant.

Challenge

The goal was to design an intuitive and always-available AI that blends naturally into designers' daily workflows without feeling intrusive or gimmicky.

Process

  • User research on AI trust and productivity needs
  • Sketching early ideas and voice interaction flows
  • Rapid prototyping with Figma and Framer
  • User testing and feedback loop iteration

Outcome

The final design is a minimal, voice-activated desktop assistant that appears subtly and integrates with calendar, moodboard, and to-do tools in a non-disruptive way.
